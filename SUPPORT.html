Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is my data private?

Yes. VoiceInk is designed with privacy as a top priority. When you use local, on-device models for transcription, your audio data never leaves your phone. If you choose to use a cloud-based transcription service, your data is sent directly to that provider. We do not store any of your audio files or transcriptions on our servers. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.

How does offline transcription work?

VoiceInk allows you to download powerful AI models directly to your device. These models run locally, which means you can get high-quality transcriptions even without an internet connection. This is perfect for maintaining privacy and for transcribing on the go.

Which transcription services are supported?

VoiceInk supports both local (offline) transcription via Whisper models and various cloud-based services like OpenAI, Groq, and Deepgram. You can enter your own API keys in the app's settings to use these providers.
