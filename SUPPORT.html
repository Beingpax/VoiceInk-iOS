Is my data private?

Yes. Privacy is a core feature of VoiceInk. When using local on-device models, your audio and transcriptions never leave your device. For cloud services, data is sent directly to the provider you've configured. We never see or store your data.

How does offline transcription work?

You can download powerful AI models directly to your device from the app's settings. Once downloaded, these models allow you to transcribe audio with high accuracy, completely offline, ensuring your privacy and enabling you to work anywhere.
